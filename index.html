Why london

London, the Capital of England.

A 21st-century city with history stretching back to Roman times. At its centre stand the imposing Houses of Parliament, the iconic ‘Big Ben’ clock tower and Westminster Abbey, site of British monarch coronations. Across the Thames River, the London Eye observation wheel provides panoramic views of the South Bank cultural complex, and the entire city.

The Tate Modern is a gallery on the banks of the thames. There are always new exibits, in all possible mediums too explore.

adress:

What I like about it

It's free entry! How often do you get that? And bursting at the seams with art of all kinds. Be amazed, be shocked, be moved, be inspired. Go and fill up your soul with beautiful feeling, for free!

The River Thames

The river thames is an Iconic landmark. It meanders through london, lined with street performers, food vendors, pop up festivals, artifacts from history and so much more. . .

What I like about it

You could walk the banks of the thames everyday for a month, and everyday, you would see something new. London is as beautiful and changeable as this delightful force of nature that carves through it.
